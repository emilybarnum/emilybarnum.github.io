Hi, I'm Emily!


I'm a senior Computer Information Systems major at James Madison University.

Over the years, I have taken courses in programming, systems analysis, database design, and interaction design. I have found that through my experiences with both technical and creative projects, my passion is front end web development. In my major capstone, I had the opportunity to work with clients and other developers to deliver a web application solution. I learned that, while I enjoy working on the backend to ensure functional requirements are met, I am much more passionate about making sure the client's requirements are presented in a way that is intuitive, user-friendly, and professional. I wanted to create this website to both showcase my ability to create a website from scratch as well as have a portfolio of all my work in one place.
